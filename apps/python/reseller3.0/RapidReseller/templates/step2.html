Step2: Create A Subscription

Every customer record can have one or more subscriptions. Subscriptions include a seat count and a SKU. For this example, a trial domain subscription will be provisioned. Additional subscriptions include paid Google Apps accounts, Google Apps Vault and optional add-on storage. Subscriptions can also auto-renew or cancel at the end of the term.

All subscriptions can be billed yearly, or on a monthly basis.
