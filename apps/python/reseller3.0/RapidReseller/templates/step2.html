Step 2: Create A Subscription

Every resold customer can have one or more subscriptions. Subscriptions include a seat count, SKU, and renewal settings. Additional subscriptions include paid Google Apps accounts, Google Apps Vault and optional add-on storage. Subscriptions can also auto-renew or cancel at the end of the term.

All subscriptions can be billed yearly, or on a monthly basis.
